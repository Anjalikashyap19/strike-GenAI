Who can apply for Udyam Registration?

Documents Required for Udyam Aadhar


Getting your Udyam Aadhaar (MSME) registration is a simple, paperless process. You only need a few essential documents to get started:

Aadhaar Card – Mandatory for the business owner, partner, or director for identity verification.

PAN Card – Required for both the applicant and the business entity.

Business Details – Name, type of organization, and date of commencement.

Bank Account Information – Account number and IFSC code.

Business Address Proof – Electricity bill, rent agreement, or property document.

Investment & Employee Details –Self-declared information on business investment and total employees.

No physical documents are needed — all verification happens online through Aadhaar and PAN. Once approved, your Udyam Registration Certificate is emailed directly to you.
