Our Simple 7-Step Registration Process

Step 1: Get Started

Click "Apply Now" or "Get Started".

Step 2: Proceed

Hit proceed on the GST Registration.

Step 3: Confirm Payment

Upon payment confirmation, you will be redirected to the documents upload page.

Step 4: Upload Documents

Submit the form after filling it out and uploading the required documents.

Step 5: Expert Assigned

After submission, a task order will be generated and assigned to our GST Expert.

Step 6: GST Calculation

Our GST Expert will calculate your monthly/quarterly GST based on your bills.

Step 7: Challan Generation

After calculation, a challan for GST payment will be generated, deducting any available ledger balance.
