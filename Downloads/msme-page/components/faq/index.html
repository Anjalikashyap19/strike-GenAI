Frequently Asked Questions

Can I apply for GST without a business?

+

Yes, individuals who meet specific criteria, such as having a certain level of income or engaging in activities that mandate GST, can register even without a formal business entity.

Is GST compulsory for small businesses?

+

GST registration is compulsory for businesses whose turnover exceeds a certain threshold limit. However, businesses can also opt for voluntary registration to avail benefits like input tax credit.

How is GST calculated?

+

GST is calculated on the transaction value of goods or services. The formula is: GST Amount = (Original Cost * GST%) / 100. The net price is then Original Cost + GST Amount.

What are the 3 types of GST registration?

+

The main types of GST registration are the Regular Scheme, for most businesses; the Composition Scheme, for small taxpayers with simplified compliance; and Voluntary Registration, for businesses that don't meet the turnover threshold but wish to register.
